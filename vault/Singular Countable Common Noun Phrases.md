```
a card
the card
1 card
one card
no card
every card
each card
not every card
not each card

all cards
no cards
```

The following determiners can be used interchangeably: 

* `a` / `1` / `one`
* `every` / `each`
* `not every` / `not each`

The noun phrase `all cards` is syntactic sugar for `every card`, and the noun phrase `no cards` is syntactic sugar for `no card`.
